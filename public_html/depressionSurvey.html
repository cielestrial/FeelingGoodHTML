Weekly Depression Survey
1. Have you been feeling sad or down?
2. Does the future look hopeless?
3. Do you feel worthless or think of yourself as a failure?
4. Do you feel inadequate or inferior to others?
5. Do you get self-critical and blame yourself?
6. Do you have trouble making up your mind?
7. Have you been feeling resentful or angry?
8. Have you lost interest in your career? Hobbies? Family? Friends?
9. Do you feel overwhelmed and have to push yourself to do things?
10. Do you feel unattractive?
11. Have you lost your appetite? Do you overeat or binge?
12. Do you find it hard to sleep well? Do you sleep too much? If you sleep well, do you still feel excessively tired?
13. Have you lost your sexual desire?
14. Do you worry about your health?
15. **Do you have thoughts that life is not worth living? That you would be better off dead?
